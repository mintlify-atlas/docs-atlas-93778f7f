## Hecho en Chitagá

Our projects are not academic exercises. They are real tools we want to build together for our municipality and its people. Each project addresses specific needs in our community, leveraging modern technology to solve local challenges.

<Note>
  All projects are built with community impact in mind, designed to serve the people of Chitagá.
</Note>

## Why These Projects?

Each project was conceived to address real needs in Chitagá:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Education Access">
    Many chitagüenses lack easy access to quality educational resources. The Educational Portal centralizes learning opportunities without depending on external platforms.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Economic Visibility">
    Local businesses and tourist attractions need better digital presence. The Digital Map puts Chitagá on the digital landscape.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Agricultural Innovation">
    Our farmers are the backbone of the community. Modern tools can help them optimize production and improve efficiency.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technology Stack

We use modern, proven technologies across our projects:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Frontend">
    - **Astro**: Fast, content-focused websites
    - **React**: Interactive user interfaces
    - **React Native**: Cross-platform mobile apps
    - **TypeScript**: Type-safe JavaScript
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Backend">
    - **Node.js**: Server-side JavaScript runtime
    - **API REST**: Standard web service architecture
    - **Firebase**: Real-time database and hosting
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Tools & Libraries">
    - **Leaflet**: Interactive mapping
    - **IoT Integration**: Sensor data collection
    - **Progressive Web Apps**: Offline-first capabilities
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
