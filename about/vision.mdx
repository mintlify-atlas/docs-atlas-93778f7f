## Where We're Headed

Our vision extends far beyond teaching technology skills. We're building a movement that transforms Chitagá into a hub of innovation and opportunity.

<Card title="Our North Star" icon="telescope">
  That from Chitagá, people can create, innovate, and transform. That the talent from here impacts not only the municipality, but Colombia and the world.
</Card>

## Creating Without Borders

Technology has demolished geographical barriers. A developer in Chitagá can build products used globally. A designer here can work with clients anywhere. Our vision is to make this reality accessible to everyone in our community.

### From Local to Global

<Steps>
  <Step title="Local Impact">
    Start by solving problems in our own community. Build digital solutions that help local businesses, improve municipal services, and connect neighbors.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Regional Reach">
    Expand our impact across Norte de Santander, creating opportunities and collaborations with nearby communities facing similar challenges.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="National Influence">
    Contribute to Colombia's tech ecosystem, showing that innovation can come from anywhere—including small towns like ours.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Global Contribution">
    Enable Chitagüenses to compete on the world stage, building products and services that transcend borders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Chitagá Tech of Tomorrow

In 5 years, we envision:

<Accordion title="A thriving tech community">
  Dozens of Chitagüenses working remotely for international companies, earning competitive salaries while living in their hometown. Regular meetups, hackathons, and collaborative projects that bring the community together.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Local startups and businesses">
  Technology businesses founded and operated in Chitagá, providing services locally and globally. Young entrepreneurs building the next generation of Colombian tech companies from right here.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Educational excellence">
  A recognized institution for tech education, attracting students not just from Chitagá but from surrounding areas. Partnerships with universities and tech companies that bring resources and opportunities to our community.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Community transformation">
  A municipality where technology improves daily life—from digital government services to smart agriculture solutions. Where innovation is part of our identity, as much as our beautiful landscapes and rich culture.
</Accordion>

## Choice Over Necessity

The ultimate measure of our success will be when leaving Chitagá is truly a choice, not a necessity.

<Note>
  When a young person decides to stay, it should be because they see a future here—not because they can't afford to leave, but because they don't want to leave behind the opportunities they've found at home.
</Note>

When someone does choose to leave, it should be with skills, confidence, and connections—knowing they can always come back to a community that values and utilizes their talents.

## Join Us in Building This Future

This vision isn't something we're building alone. It requires:

- **Students** willing to learn and grow
- **Professionals** ready to mentor and share knowledge  
- **Community members** who believe in local potential
- **Businesses** willing to invest in local talent
- **Leaders** committed to supporting innovation

Every person who participates in Chitagá Tech becomes part of this vision, helping write the next chapter of our municipality's story.
