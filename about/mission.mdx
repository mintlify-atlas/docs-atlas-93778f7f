## Why We Exist

In Chitagá, there is no place to learn the skills this century demands. Many young people leave because they don't find opportunities here. Others stay without the tools to compete.

**Chitagá Tech was born to change that.**

<Note>
  Leaving shouldn't be the only option. We're creating opportunities so people can choose to stay.
</Note>

## Our Core Mission

Chitagá Tech is a physical and digital space created by professionals from our municipality, where anyone can learn technology without having to leave their homeland.

## The Problem We're Solving

The reality in Chitagá is clear:

- **No local tech education**: There's nowhere to learn programming, web design, or digital skills
- **Forced migration**: Young people leave not by choice, but because there are no opportunities
- **Skills gap**: Those who stay lack the tools to compete in the modern economy
- **Lost potential**: Talented individuals don't get the chance to develop their abilities

## Making It Real, Not Just Theory

<Warning>
  This is not just basic computer literacy. We're preparing our people with the skills that open doors anywhere in the world.
</Warning>

Our mission centers on **real training** through:

- **Practical courses**: Programming, web design, and digital tools from zero with real projects
- **Hands-on workshops**: Short, practical sessions where you learn by doing
- **Community gatherings**: Spaces to connect with other Chitagüenses who share the same vision
- **Mentorship**: Professionals from Chitagá who've already walked the path accompany you

## Your Future Shouldn't Depend on Leaving

At the heart of our mission is a simple belief: staying in Chitagá should be a choice, not a limitation. Technology creates opportunities that transcend geography, and we're bringing those opportunities home.

<Accordion title="Why this matters to our community">
  When young people have to leave to pursue their dreams, our community loses its future. Families are separated, local knowledge is lost, and the cycle continues. By providing world-class tech education right here in Chitagá, we're not just teaching skills—we're building a future where our community can thrive without being forced to scatter.
</Accordion>
