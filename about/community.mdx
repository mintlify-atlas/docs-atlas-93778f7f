## De Chitagüenses, Para Chitagüenses

Chitagá Tech is fundamentally a **community initiative**. We're not an external organization bringing solutions from elsewhere. We're professionals from Chitagá who've succeeded in tech and are now bringing those opportunities home.

<Note>
  Built by Chitagüenses, for Chitagüenses. This is our space, created with love for our municipality and commitment to its future.
</Note>

## Creating Spaces for Connection

Technology can be isolating, but at Chitagá Tech, we prioritize **human connection**:

### Physical & Digital Presence

We're building both a **physical space** where people can meet, learn, and collaborate, and a **digital platform** that extends our reach to every corner of the municipality.

<Accordion title="The physical space">
  A welcoming environment in Chitagá where students can access computers, internet, and most importantly—each other. A place to study, work on projects, get help from mentors, and build relationships with others on the same journey.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The digital platform">
  Online courses, resources, and community forums accessible from anywhere in the municipality. Whether you live in the center of town or in a rural vereda, you can participate in Chitagá Tech.
</Accordion>

### Community Events

Regular gatherings strengthen bonds and create opportunities:

- **Learning circles**: Small groups meeting regularly to work through courses together
- **Project showcases**: Students presenting what they've built to the community
- **Tech talks**: Local professionals sharing their experiences and knowledge
- **Collaboration sessions**: Working together on projects that benefit Chitagá
- **Celebration events**: Recognizing achievements and milestones together

## Staying Connected to Our Roots

As we grow and learn technology, we never lose sight of what makes Chitagá special:

<Note>
  Technology is a tool, not an end in itself. We use it to strengthen our community, preserve our culture, and build a better future while staying true to our identity.
</Note>

### Technology + Tradition

We see opportunities to use technology to:

- **Preserve local culture**: Digital archives of our history, traditions, and stories
- **Support local businesses**: Help traditional businesses reach new markets through digital tools
- **Improve agriculture**: Bring modern techniques and data to our farming community
- **Enhance tourism**: Showcase Chitagá's beauty and culture to the world
- **Strengthen governance**: Enable better communication between citizens and local government

## Everyone Is Welcome

Chitagá Tech is for **everyone** in our community:

- **Students** looking for career opportunities
- **Working adults** seeking new skills or career changes  
- **Entrepreneurs** wanting to bring their ideas to life
- **Seniors** curious about technology
- **Business owners** needing digital tools
- **Community leaders** exploring tech solutions

<Accordion title="No prerequisites needed">
  You don't need previous experience, expensive equipment, or advanced education. Just curiosity and commitment. We provide the computers, internet, instruction, and support. You bring the willingness to learn.
</Accordion>

## Building Together

The strength of Chitagá Tech is our community. We're not customers or clients—we're neighbors, friends, and family building something together.

- **Share your expertise**: Whatever you know can help someone
- **Provide feedback**: Help us improve and better serve the community
- **Participate in events**: Your presence builds community
- **Support students**: Encouragement goes a long way
